HP Envy 6000 Series - User Manual / Troubleshooting

1. Light Status
- Solid White: Printer is ready.
- Blinking White: Printing in progress.
- Solid Amber: Printer is initializing or in sleep mode.
- Blinking Amber: Attention required (Paper jam, Out of paper, or Cover open).
- Fast Blinking Amber: Serious error state. Turn printer off and on again.
- Blue Light: Wi-Fi connecting or active.

2. Common Actions
- Print Test Page: Hold 'cancel' and 'resume' buttons for 3 seconds.
- Reset Wi-Fi: Hold the 'Wi-Fi' button on the back for 5 seconds until the light blinks blue.

3. Error Codes (See orders.db for more details)
- E1: Paper width mismatch.
- E3: Carriage jam.
- E4: Paper jam.
